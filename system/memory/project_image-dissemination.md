William wants the widest possible image dissemination. The paintings are "iconic" abstractions — they communicate their imagery even at tiny sizes (phone screens, thumbnails, social previews). In-person impact is still different, but digital reproduction doesn't meaningfully diminish the work.

**Why:** Discovery is driven by press and social sharing. Images must be everywhere. HC-01 IP retention means William still owns reproduction rights — allowing editorial/promotional use doesn't give that up.

**How to apply:** Press gets a liberal editorial-use license. Social link previews should show painting images prominently. Gallery scroller thumbnails will be effective because of the iconic abstraction style. No need for "images don't do it justice" disclaimers — the images work.
